About Me

LCG member

    I was born in a remote area of ​​Shanxi Province, China. When I was a child, provided that I played with the children who played together would be exceedingly active, but when I met strangers, I would be definitely shy. In the blink of an eye, twenty years have passed, but my whole person is actually numb. "The trees are still there, but that people are no more the same ones." This emotion is overwhelmingly heartbreaking. When I am writing this article, I dreamed a girl who I liked in my junior school last night. The girl's face I have forgotten, but her voice fascinated me in a dream. When I wake up, all things disappeared. I can solely lie quietly in bed, close my eyes and carefully relive that feeling. It seems that I am still the young boy who doesn't require thinking much.

    When I first came into contact with programming, it was the second year of junior high school. Then I started to be absorbed in computers, gradually started to contact and understand hackers, and felt that they were very cool. Since then, I have firmly vowed to take the computer's road. After going to college, although the test scores are slightly better.But contrasted to some good students, I feel that I am still a lot worse. On this road, I feel that I will not meet people who can always with me, perhaps because the forest is small, or perhaps the people with lofty aspirations can't look at me in the same trade .I always feel that I am very embarrassed to talk to others about my lofty ideals. So I am not good at telling others I hide I deeply have deeply felt in my heart until one day can be realized. I hope I never have the feeling that reaching for what is beyond one's grasp. I can do everything in practice.I only hope that I will walk down the ground until one day my group of molecules is dissolved.
